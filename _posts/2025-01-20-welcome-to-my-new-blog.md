I'm excited to launch my newly redesigned personal site and blog. After years of sharing my writing primarily on Medium, I wanted to create a home for my own thoughts and ideas.

## Why a Personal Blog?

While I love platforms like Medium for their reach and community, there's something special about having your own space on the web. A place where you have complete control over the design, the content, and the experience.

This redesign focuses on what matters most: **typography and readability**. Clean lines, generous whitespace, and a focus on the written word.

## What to Expect

I'll be sharing thoughts on:

- **Technology and civic innovation** — How we can use technology to improve our cities and communities
- **Media and journalism** — The evolving landscape of local news and community engagement
- **Building communities** — Lessons from WhereBy.Us, Letterhead, and other projects
- **Miami and cities** — Thoughts on urban life, especially in emerging cities
