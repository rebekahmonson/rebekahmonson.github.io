If you're reading this, welcome to the new rebekahmonson.com!

I've been meaning to rebuild this site for... a while. The old one was a single-page Jekyll site that I built in 2016 and barely touched since. It served its purpose, but it didn't feel like *me* anymore. And if your personal site doesn't feel like you, what's the point?

## Why now?

A few reasons:

- **I want to write more.** I've spent years building products and platforms for other people to publish on. It's time I got back to publishing myself. Expect weekly-ish posts here on media, technology, community building, and whatever else is on my mind.

- **Consulting and speaking.** I'm leaning into more advisory and speaking work, and I needed a home base that reflects what I actually do now &mdash; not what I did five years ago.

- **It's fun.** Building things is what I do. Why not build something for myself?

## What to expect

I'll be writing about the things I spend my days thinking about:

- The future of local news and community media
- Newsletter strategy and audience engagement
- Building sustainable media businesses
- Civic technology and community building
- Lessons from building startups

If any of that sounds interesting, stick around. And if you want to work together on something, [let's talk](/contact/).

Here's to new chapters.
