About

Rebekah Monson is co-founder and VP of Product and Editorial for WhereBy.Us. With more than a decade of experience in journalism, technology, online media and design, she is an active collaborator with a proven ability to manage complex problems, high-achieving teams, tight deadlines and challenging business conditions.
